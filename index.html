Wei Song

Professor
Information Engineering College
Capital Normal University, China

email: wsong at cnu.edu.cn

I'm professor in the Information Engineering College at Capital Normal University (CNU). I am working on natural language processing, information retrieval and their practical applications. The target is to let computer understand language and use language for evaluation, reasoning and prediction. I am also interested in digital humanity, that exploits computational techniques to help appreciate and criticize literature.

Before joining CNU, I received my PhD at the social computing and information retrieval center, Harbin Institute of Technology. I received my master and bachelor degrees from the school of computer science, Harbin Institute of Technology.
