Natural Language Processing at Capital Normal University (CNU), China

Wei Song

Professor at the College of Information Engineering at CNU.

He received his Ph.D. degree at the Social Computing and Information Retrieval (SCIR) Center and received his master's and bachelor's degrees from the School of Computer Science, Harbin Institute of Technology.

Research interests: natural language processing (NLP), including figurative language processing, discourse analysis, and educational applications of NLP.

email: wsong at cnu.edu.cn
